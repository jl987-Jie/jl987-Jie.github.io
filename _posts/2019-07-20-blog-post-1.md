### Notes from book "Reinforcement Learning - An Introduction" by Sutton and Barto

### Policy Gradient

\begin{equation}
V_\theta(s) \approx V^{\pi}(s)
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
Q_\theta(s, a) \approx Q^{\pi}(s, a)
\end{equation}

Policy based RL: policy generated from value function using $\epsilon$-greedy.
